How to say a hand

Say your cards in order, and make the dealer’s upcard the last one you say. No need to say the word “dealer.”

Examples:
“king nine seven” — you have K, 9; dealer 7
“ace seven six” — you have A, 7; dealer 6
“eight eight ten” — you have 8, 8; dealer 10
“five six ace” — you have 5, 6; dealer ace

Saying “dealer” before the last card still works if you prefer, but leaving it out is more reliable.

Face cards and ten all count as 10. Two matching cards are read as a pair for splits. An ace is treated as a soft hand automatically.

Rules used: 6 decks, dealer stands on soft 17, double after split allowed.
